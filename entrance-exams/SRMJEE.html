SRMJEE PROCESS

Interested candidates can apply for SRMJEEE 2017 in the following way:

Apply Online

Apply online with e-payment: Aspirants need to visit https://applications.srmuniv.ac.in and apply online.Please note that physical applications (Hard Copy) are not allowed. It is not sold at any point of sales within or outside the campus. The application cum entrance examination fee is Rs. 1060/- per slot.

Online with Demand Draft: Application to be filled online by visiting https://applications.srmuniv.ac.in/ and the same can be downloaded and sent to the university with the DD for Rs.`1060/- drawn in favour of SRMIST, payable at Chennai. This has to reach us before the last date specified. Candidates should write their name and address on the reverse of DD and send to:
The Director,
The Directorate of Admissions,
SRM University, Kattankulathur,
Kancheepuram District, Tamil Nadu 603203.
Your application will be processed only upon receipt of the DD.

Candidates are advised to go through a set of detailed general instruction which are listed on SRM University’s official website.

Selection Procedure:

Aspirants are invited for a counseling session which is based on the score obtained in SRMJEEE (UG) 2017
SRM may conduct one or more counseling sessions depending on the vacancy of seats.
Aspirants are required to choose their preferred specialization / branch of study in a particular campus based on:

(a) the cut-off score for the specified specialization / branch.
(b) fulfilling the programme-specific eligibility criteria for a particular campus and
(c) subject to the availability of seats at that point of time.

If a candidate falls short of the cut-off score for a preferred specialization / branch for a particular campus, the candidate can choose to retake the SRMJEEE (UG) to improve the individual score, subject to a maximum of TWO retakes. In that case the best score out of the three scores (first attempt + 2 retakes) will be considered while choosing/allocating the preferred specialization / branch, subject to the conditions laid above.

A one-time programme sliding within the campus is permitted subject to availability of seats and eligibility based on cut-off score. The sliding will be considered, at appropriate time as decided by the university, ONLY for those candidates who have submitted the option to be considered for sliding.

Full tuition fees and other applicable fees required to be paid at the time of counseling after aspirants select their choice of specialization / branch.

Students will recive a Provisional Allotment Letter (PAL) to confirm their admission.

SRMJEE ELIGIBILITY

SRMJEEE 2017 eligibility criteria require candidates to fulfill multiple aspects if they want to appear for this engineering entrance exam.

Aspirants must be a Resident Indian, Non-Resident Indian (NRI), holder of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India. NRIs, holders of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India who have not taken the SRMJEEE (UG) must apply under International student category only.

Candidate must have completed the age of 17 years as on December 31 of the admission year.

Faculty of Engineering and Technology:

All B.Tech programmes:

For Kattankulathur, Ramapuram and Vadapalani campuses : Minimum 60 % aggregate in PCM
For NCR, Delhi campus : Minimum 55 % aggregate in PCM

(i)Passed in Higher secondary examination (10+2 pattern ) or appearing in Higher Secondary examination in the current academic year with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as major subjects in regular stream from any state board within India, CBSE, ISCE, Matriculation, or NIOS.

(ii)International Baccalaureate (IB) with Diploma, or A-level with Mathematics, Physics and chemistry as major subjects in any International schools within India

B.Tech Biotechnology, B.Tech Biomedical Engineering and B.Tech Genetic Engineering:

For Kattankulathur, Ramapuram and Vadapalani campuses : Minimum 60 % aggregate in PCM / PCB
For NCR, Delhi campus : Minimum 55 % aggregate in PCM / PCB

(i) Passed in Higher secondary examination (10+2 pattern ) or appearing in Higher Secondary examination in the current academic year with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics or Biology or Biotechnology as major subjects in regular stream from any state board within India, CBSE, ISCE, Matriculation, or NIOS

(ii)International Baccalaureate (IB) with Diploma, or A-level with Physics, chemistry, Mathematics or Biology as major subjects in any International schools within India

Note: Only those Candidates who have passed the above qualifying examination in not more than 2 attempts including appearance for improvement will be considered for admission
