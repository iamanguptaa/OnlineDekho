Designing Entrance Exams

NID

National Institute of Design (NID) offers admissions to aspirants in Bachelor of Design (BDes) as well as Master of Design (MDes) programmes on the basis of their performance in NID Design Aptitude Test.

LEARN MORE

NIFT

National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) conducts NIFT entrance exam in order to shortlist aspirants for admission in design programmes offered by it.


LEARN MORE

UCEED

Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay conducts Undergraduate Common Entrance Examination for Design 2017 (UCEED 2017) under guidance of the UCEED Admissions Committee 2017.

LEARN MORE

CEED

CEED is conducted by IIT Bombay for admissions in Master of Design & PhD programmes offered at its campus as well as at IIT Delhi, IISc Bangalore, IIT Guwahati, IIT Kanpur and IIT Hyderabad.
