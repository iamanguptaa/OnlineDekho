eLitmus

eLitmus Evaluation Private Limited is an Indian company that helps companies in hiring employees for entry-level jobs.It was founded in the year 2005 by former employees of Infosys. Some of the Fortune 500 companies hire their employees through the pH test conducted by eLitmus.

pH Test or Hiring Potential Test is an assessment exam conducted by eLitmus.Companies use pH Test as a criterion for hiring employees. The test consists of three parts- the Quantitative section, the Verbal section and the Reasoning section. Results are declared in two formats: percentile score and total score.Companies call test-takers for interviews on the basis of pH score and their own criteria.

AMCAT

The AMCAT is a computer adaptive test which measures job applicants on critical areas like communication skills, logical reasoning, quantitative skills and job specific domain skills thus helping recruiters identify the suitability of a candidate.While most aptitude tests only measure a test taker's verbal comprehension and reasoning abilities, the AMCAT additionally evaluates personality traits and domain skills, thus becoming an ideal test to match jobs to candidates.

The AMCAT is fast emerging as an industry benchmark with 700+ companies using it as a compulsory testing mechanism for entry level roles. We work alongside industry leading names like Accenture, Snapdeal, Axis Bank, Tata Motors, ITC and many more. NASSCOM Nac-Tech

NAC TECH test is organized by NASSCOM and is one of the best employability tests in India. It helps in increasing the employability of job seekers. It is conducted by Merit Trac Services that has partnership with NASSCOM.

It is a national employability test and those who pass this test are eligible to attend interview calls from employers. The test is of 3 hours long and consists of 2 parts viz. PART A and PART B. PART A comprises various sections viz. verbal ability, analytical ability, attention to detail, written English test, programming fundamentals and learning ability.
